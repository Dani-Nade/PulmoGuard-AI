    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

    What is PulmoGuard AI?

    PulmoGuard AI is a lung-cancer risk prediction system that analyzes your scan images using a trained machine-learning model to estimate your risk level.

    How does the Lung-Cancer Risk Predictor work?

    You upload your CT or X-ray scan; our model extracts features and returns a probability score along with an explanation of contributing factors.

    What data does the system require?

    We require a clear lung scan image (CT or X-ray) plus basic patient information like age and smoking history to generate an accurate prediction.

    Which file formats are supported?

    We accept DICOM (.dcm), JPEG, and PNG formats. Please ensure the scan is high resolution for best results.

    How long does a prediction take?

    Predictions typically complete within 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on image size and server load.

    How accurate are the predictions?

    Our model achieves over 85% overall accuracy on held-out clinical datasets; individual results may vary based on image quality.

    Can I trust the confidence score?

    The confidence score reflects model certainty; a higher score indicates stronger evidence for the predicted outcome.

    How often is the model updated?

    We retrain and redeploy the model quarterly with new validated data to continually improve performance.

    What is the false positive/negative rate?

    Current validation shows a false-positive rate around 10% and a false-negative rate below 8%, corroborated by clinical experts.

    How can I help improve accuracy?

    You can submit feedback on any prediction via the “Report an issue” button; this data helps us refine the model.

    How is my data protected?

    All uploads are encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest, stored on secure, HIPAA-compliant servers.

    Does the system store my scans?

    We retain scans only as long as necessary for audit and improvement; you may request deletion at any time.

    Who can access my medical data?

    Only you and your authorized healthcare providers can view your results—PulmoGuard AI staff do not access personal data.

    Is my data anonymized?

    All personal identifiers are stripped before model training; only de-identified data is used for research.

    Can I delete my data?

    Yes—visit your account settings and click “Delete my data.” We’ll remove all scans and results within 30 days.

    Do I need an account to use the predictor?

    Yes—creating an account lets you save scan history, receive notifications, and manage your data securely.

    How do I view my scan history?

    Go to “Scan History” in the sidebar to see all past predictions, dates, and confidence scores.

    Can I export my results?

    Yes—you can download a PDF report for each scan from the “Result Details” page.

    How do I interpret the risk level?

    Risk levels are categorized as Low, Moderate, or High based on probability thresholds defined by clinical guidelines.

    Where can I get help or support?

    Visit the “Contact Us” page or click the chat icon for 24/7 support from our PulmoGuard AI team.
